Veg Grilled Sandwich

This Veg Grilled Sandwich is a tasty recipe for breakfast, snacks or a light meal. It is easy to make and full of fresh vegetables.

Ingredients

  • Bread
  • Butter
  • Sliced Tomatoes
  • Sliced Onions
  • Sliced Cucumber
  • Boiled Potatoes
  • Cheese Slices(Optional)

Instructions

  1. Butter both sides of two bread slices.
  2. layer Potatoes, Cucumbers, Tomatoes, Onions, and Cheese on one slice of bread.
  3. Sprinkle a pinch of chaat masala for extra flavor.
  4. Close the sandwich and grill until golden brown and crisp.
